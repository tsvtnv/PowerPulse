## ✨ What is this?

The website for Power Pulse Solutions — a professional jet washing and pressure washing service based in Sutton Coldfield. Built to convert visitors into booked jobs with a direct call CTA and online quote form. Open-sourced as a portfolio example.

## 🔥 Features

- **📋 Online Quote Form** — Instant enquiry submission for prospective clients
- **📞 24/7 Call CTA** — Prominent call-to-action throughout the page
- **🗂 6 Service Categories** — Driveways, patios, roofs, gutters, cars, and commercial
- **⭐ Testimonials** — Trust-building social proof and before/after gallery
- **📍 Local SEO** — Optimised for Sutton Coldfield and surrounding areas
